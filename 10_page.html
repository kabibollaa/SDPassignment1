About Us

AutoDealer Kazakhstan - A New Way to Buy Used Cars

AutoDealer Kazakhstan makes buying a pre-owned vehicle easy. Our 1Price Pre-Owned vehicles feature a low, haggle-free price and come with a Money-Back Guarantee*. Shop certified vehicles that have been checked and inspected in 125 different places. Plus, we'll buy your car, no purchase necessary. Now that's easy.

Part of the process in our transformative approach involved us tossing out everything we knew about traditional car sales. Instead, we developed a dialogue directly with our customers.

At USA Auto Dealer the customer always comes first. We are an established dealership with years of experience in the automotive industry that provides customers with a complete automotive experience at competitive prices. Through great customer service and honest pricing, we have earned the trust and respect of Manteca and the surrounding San Joaquin County communities.

We accept all types of credit. Good, Fair, & even Bad credit! Trust our team to work out a great deal to fit your budget and credit situation.

We go above and beyond to make sure that we hand select only reliable inventory. We are passionate about luxury cars and we intend on sharing that passion with you when you purchase your new car from us.

From hassle-free shopping to easy financing, we provide you with a great car buying experience when purchasing a used vehicle from USA Auto Dealer. We carry a wide variety of makes and models to choose from
