More info

One of the rarest versions in the Mercedes-Benz W140 model range was the Mercedes-Benz S600L 7.0 AMG (or S70 AMG).

In 1991, Mercedes-Benz introduced the legendary M120 engine with a displacement of 6.0 liters, which was installed on all S600 (W140), CL600 (C140) and SL600 (R129).

The appearance of this engine enabled AMG to create a number of modifications with a volume of 6.9, 7.1 and 7.3 liters.
